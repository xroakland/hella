XRO Mission

The purpose of XRO is to elicit an appropriate response to the climate crisis. It's impossible to tell when we have passed the tipping points of no return until it's too late. Tipping points result in extreme, abrupt climate change and they are well documented in peer-reviewed literature.

Our house is on fire, but our elected officials aren't acting like it. At this time, the only human on Earth telling the truth about abrupt climate change is a 16 year old (Ms. Greta Thunberg). Righteous Greta has gone above and beyond while our leaders sit idle in some kind of Disneyland Fairytale. XRO wants our elected officials to stop being inappropriate, and act like the truth is real.

The best roadmap to real change is nonviolent and respectful civil disobedience. XRO will be doing weekly "headed for extinction and what to do about it" talks in 2020. Could be as soon as January. It's a lot of work so please get in touch if you want to help

Love and Rage

At this time, America is being destroyed by the fossil fuel industry. Brazenly manufactured doubt is ubiquitous. They have effectively dismantled academia and bona fide scientific research. In turn, the amount of American 'flat-earthers' has rapidly increased.

Simply put, the situation is unacceptable. It is time to put pressure on our leaders. Increase reputational costs until they wake up and do their jobs.

Providing a livable future for the next generation is the golden rule of all societies. Henceforth, XRO's proposal: if it's still 'business-as-usual' this spring, then thousands of young people will mobilize into San Francisco. It will begin on a specific date, and continue each and every day until meaningful legislation takes place.
Make no mistake- in a situation like this, civil disobedience will mean doing the right thing.
It's civic duty. We have obligations to fulfill. Our future is being stolen right in front of our face. Science denial is unacceptable.
We will shut the city down until our elected officials act like the truth is real.
