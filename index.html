Civic Duty

The purpose of XRO is to elicit an appropriate response to the climate crisis. At this time, It's impossible to tell if we have already passed temperature tipping points. Tipping points cause runaway, irreversible heating of our atmosphere and they are well documented in peer-reviewed literature.

Our house is on fire, but our elected officials aren't acting like it.
The climate emergency is an emergency, but the only human on Earth warning human kind is 17 years old. Greta Thunberg has gone above and beyond while our leaders disregard basic reality.
XRO wants our leaders to start pulling fire alarms and emergency brakes. Because that's what one does in an emergency.
The climate crisis is real, our house is on fire, but being an alarmist is frowned upon. #Profundity


Right now, there are cowardly billionaire fossil-fools building doomsday bunkers.
Guess we should just keep our fingers crossed and hope that a miracle happens? Maybe we should go buy green lightbulbs and solar panels and recycle empty cans? No, all of those are ineffective (and silly).
It's an emergency!
Hoping and praying, virtue-signaling (and shaming), are both inappropriate in life-or-death situations. Like being trapped in a house fire.
So if personal accountability, consumer choice is unhelpful, then what are we to do? How can we respond effectively to the climate crisis?
Here's how: mass participation nonviolent civil disobedience.
XRO was created to do exactly that.
The rebellion is just getting started so please get in touch if you want to help!

Love and Rage

At this time, America is being destroyed by the fossil fuel industry. Brazenly manufactured doubt is ubiquitous. They have effectively dismantled academia and bona fide scientific research. In turn, the amount of American 'flat-earthers' has rapidly increased.

Simply put, the situation is unacceptable. It is time to put pressure on our leaders. Increase their reputational costs to incentivize meaningful legislation

Our goverment is ignoring the implications of our dire predicament. This isn't new, however. At our childrens peril, co2 levels were over 400 ppm years before Trump got elected. Meaningful legislation against emitters has not happened for decades.
Hence, XRO's proposal: if it's still 'business-as-usual' this spring, then thousands of young people will mobilize into San Francisco. It will begin on a specific date, and continue each and every day until meaningful legislation is on the books.
Make no mistake- in this situation, civil disobedience is absolutely necessary. Nonviolent, respectful civil disobedience will surely be considered doing the right thing.
Awareness of the climate crisis comes with the responsibility to do something about it. A habitable future is being stolen right in front of our face.
What kind of people are we, if we cant protect our children?
We kindly ask our elected officials to Act now, or we will shut the city down with respectful nonviolence until you behave as if the truth is real.
