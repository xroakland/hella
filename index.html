Goal

The goal of XRO is to elicit an appropriate response to the climate crisis. Our house is on fire, but our elected officials aren't acting like our house is on fire.

Love and Rage

At this time, America is being destroyed by the fossil fuel industry. Their manufactured doubt is brazenly ubiquitous. They have all but dismantled Academia and bona fide scientific research. In turn, the amount of American 'flat-earthers' has gone through the roof.

Simply put, our situation is unacceptable. It is time to put pressure on our leaders. Increase reputational costs until they are compelled to do their jobs.

Providing a livable future for the next generation is the golden rule of all societies. Henceforth, XRO's proposal: if it's still 'business-as-usual' this spring, thousands of young people will mobilize into SF streets day after day after day. We will shut down the city until they act like the truth is real.

"To be afraid is to act as if the truth were not true" Bayard Rustin

"Justice too long delayed, is justice denied" Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr.

The Sad Truth

There is no guarantee that nonviolent tactics will produce meaningful change. What we can be certain of, however, is that trusting the government to meaningfully respond to the climate crisis = a death sentence. Governments around the world have known about climate change for decades. There has been zero meaningful action. It seems they have too much skin in the game. The main role of government is to plan for the future. Apparently, they have failed. "Safety Power Shutoffs" are merely a symptom of the illness.
